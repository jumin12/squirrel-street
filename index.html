How To Play

  • Tap the on-screen D-pad, drag the joystick, or use the arrow keys/WASD to guide your squirrel through traffic.
  • Collect glowing acorns—letter acorns stock your word pool, and special acorns drop the powerups you need to finish the level.
  • Reach the treetop Word Forge to spell words, stash favorites, and cash in any bonus letter picks you brought along.
  • Every valid word adds to your score—letter values and combo bonuses feed multipliers for word length and rare letters.
  • Need to bail? Skipping keeps your current score, stashed letters, and any bonus letters you've earned.
  • Higher levels crank up car speed and raise the letter quota, so plan your crossings carefully.
  • Score milestones (every 12,000 points) award an extra life automatically—keep that combo rolling.

Powerup Acorns

Blue — Time Slow

Slows traffic to a crawl for about 3 seconds while you stay nimble—perfect for risky crossings.

Red — Random Letter

Banks a weighted bonus letter for the Word Forge—it appears automatically when you spell.

Green — Letter Choice

Add any letter you want when the Word Forge opens. Ideal for finishing long words or covering rare consonants.

Gold — Extra Life

Gain a bonus life instantly. Perfect for surviving the late-level rush.

Bonus letters reset after each level and whenever you lose a life. Extra lives come from gold acorns, 12,000-point score milestones, and shop upgrades—keep them safe!

Scoring Basics

Letters: Letter acorns award +1 point on pickup and add their tile to your Word Forge pool.

Words: Word score = (10 base + letter values + combo bonuses). Letter values follow Scrabble-style weights (common letters 1 pt, J/X 8 pts, Q/Z 10 pts) and combos like TH/ING add extra points.

Multipliers: The subtotal is multiplied by 1 + 0.14 per letter beyond three and by 1 + 0.18 for each rare letter (J, Q, X, Z, K).

Powerups: Bonus letters from red or green acorns reduce the final word score by 12% each (capped at 48%).

Bonus: +12 points for every extra word you make in the same level.

Milestones: Every 12,000 points earned grants an automatic extra life.

Diamond Acorns 💎

Earned at the end of each run:

• Base: 1 diamond
• Level Progress: +0.5 per level cleared (total reward rounded down when the run ends)
• Words Spelled: +1 per word
• Letter Bonus: +1 per 5 letters spelled

Spell more words to earn more diamonds! Rewards are rounded down, so every extra level or word counts. Use them in the Shop for upgrades.
